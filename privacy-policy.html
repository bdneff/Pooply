Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 23, 2026  •  Last Updated: January 23, 2026

BetSlips LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Pooply mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using Pooply, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Account Information:

  • Name
  • Age
  • Weight
  • Biological sex
  • Email address (if provided)

Health and Wellness Data:

  • Bowel movement logs (frequency, type, characteristics)
  • Bristol Stool Scale classifications
  • Hydration levels
  • Fiber intake estimates
  • Blood presence indicators
  • Timestamps of entries
  • Notes and observations

Questionnaire Responses:

  • Digestive health history
  • Dietary preferences
  • Health goals
  • Bowel frequency patterns

1.2 Images and Photographs

When you use the camera feature, we collect and store photographs you upload. These images become the property of BetSlips LLC and may be used for:

  • AI-powered analysis and scoring
  • Machine learning model training and improvement
  • Research and development
  • Service enhancement
  • Any other lawful business purpose

Images are retained indefinitely and may be used even after your account is deleted.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App version

Usage Data:

  • Features accessed
  • Time spent in App
  • Interaction patterns
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

Location Data:

  • We do not collect precise location data
  • General region may be inferred from device settings

2.4 Research and Development

  • Conducting anonymized health research

  • Developing new wellness features

2.5 Legal and Safety

  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service

  • Ensuring user safety

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your information, including:

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Export

You can access your data within the App and export it in CSV or JSON format.

6.2 Correction

You can update your profile information at any time through the App.

6.3 Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. Note that:

  • Photographs remain property of BetSlips LLC and may not be deleted

  • Anonymized or aggregated data derived from your information may be retained

6.4 Push Notifications

You can opt out of push notifications through your device settings.

6.5 Do Not Track

The App does not respond to "Do Not Track" signals.

  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@pooply.app.

Our legal bases for processing include consent, contract performance, legitimate interests, and legal compliance.
